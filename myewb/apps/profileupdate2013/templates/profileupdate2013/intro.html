Welcome back to myEWB, and thank you for participating in the first-ever EWB Census!

Engineers Without Borders has a network of over 40,000 members, donors, and supporters - and we are trying to find new and better ways to keep you connected to this network.

At the same time, we are finding that our information is more and more outdated ... which makes it difficult to ensure that your EWB experience is tailored to your needs and interests.

So ... we are introducing the EWB Census! We want to make sure our information is up-to-date, in order to stay in touch with you and find out about what you are interested in.

This should only take 5-10 minutes - let's get started!
